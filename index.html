The Liouville equation

\[ \frac{\partial \hat{\rho}}{\partial t} = \frac{-i}{\hbar} \left[ \hat{\mathcal{H}}(t), \hat{\rho} \right] - \hat{\mathcal{D}}[\hat{\rho}] \]
  • Extension of Schrodinger eqn. for mixed states
  • Allows T1, T2 decay times
\[ \hat{\mathcal{H}}(t) = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & \hbar \chi(t) \\ \hbar \chi^*(t) & \hbar \omega_0 \end{pmatrix} \]
\[ \hat{\mathcal{D}}[\hat{\rho}] = \begin{pmatrix} (\rho_{00} - 1)/T_1 & \rho_{01}/T_2 \\ \rho_{10}/T_2 & \rho_{11}/T_1 \end{pmatrix} \]
